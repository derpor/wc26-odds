Paste a probability table (one team per row). Each row needs the team name and its cumulative reach-stage probabilities. The parser reads the last five percentages on each line as Reach R16, QF, SF, Final, Champion — so leading columns like "top group" and "L32 (round of 32)" are ignored automatically. Then click Parse & use and switch Source to "Custom".
Data source: Polymarket via its public Gamma API (free, real-time market prices). Stage odds: decimal odds = 1 / market price; hover a cell for the implied price. Outcome probabilities: mutually exclusive final placements per team, summing to 100%. Computed as differences of stage probabilities (e.g. out in QF = P(reach SF is false | reached QF path) = P(QF) − P(SF)). 3rd vs 4th place are assumed equally likely (no separate market). "Out before R16" includes both group-stage exits and Round-of-32 exits — Polymarket has no separate "reach Round of 32" market in the 48-team format. Stage prices are clamped to be non-increasing across rounds, and rows are renormalized so categories sum to exactly 100%. Expected payouts: probability-weighted payout per team using the scheme 200 / 100 / 50 / 25 / 10 / 5 / 0 (champion → out before R16). Market making: bid = 0.9 × EV, ask = 1.1 × EV. A "Won't get past" cap conditions the outcome distribution on the team not advancing beyond that stage (e.g. cap = Quarterfinal ⇒ P(4th/3rd/2nd/1st) = 0, remaining buckets rescaled to 100%); alternative EV and quotes are computed from the conditioned distribution. Net positions and caps persist in this browser only (localStorage) and survive refreshes. Consistent probabilities (IPF): when enabled, iterative proportional fitting rescales the outcome matrix so every team's row sums to 100% AND each column matches tournament structure (exactly 1 champion, 1 runner-up, 1 third, 1 fourth, 4 QF losers, 8 R16 losers, 32 out before R16). Raw market prices violate these (e.g. 2nd-place probabilities can sum to ~140% across teams due to bookmaker-style margin in each market). EV, bids and asks follow whichever mode is selected. Not betting advice.
